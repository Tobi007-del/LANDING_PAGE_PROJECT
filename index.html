Features

These features help streamline and manage purchasing activities, supplier relationships, and overall supply chain management

Bidding

The bidding feature enables the organization to solicit and evluate competitive bids from multiple suppliers for the required goods or services. Suppliers can submit their bids throught the system.

Customized Notification

The customized notification feature allows users to receive tailored alerts and updates based on their preferences and roles. Send notifications such as new bid submissions, approval status changes, and delivery updates.

Vendors

The vendors feature manages the information and interactions with suppliers.

Approval Process

The approval process feature ensures that procurement activities go through approvals.

Product Category

The product category feature organizes products and services into categories for easier management and procurement. Ensure standardized naming and classification of products to reduce confusion and errors.

Report

The reporting feature provides insiths and analytics on various procurement activities.
